BELLEVUE RESORT

BARANGAY DOLJO, PANGLAO ISLAND, BOHOL

OPEN 24 HOURS

INFORMATION

Escape to your own private sanctuary in The Bellevue Resort in Panglao, Philippines. Situated on the serene shores of north Bohol, this Panglao Island resort is an exclusive 5-star resort placing emphasis on your comfort and privacy. The Bellevue Resort is an ASEAN Green Hotel awardee. Eco-friendly principles and energy conservation measures are in place to ensure sustainable tourism at this hotel. Rest assured that you will not be damaging Mother Earth whenever you stay at The Bellevue Resort.

Located on the coastline of Panglao Island, 4.1 mi from Alona Beach and 6.5 mi from Hinagdanan Cave, The Bellevue Resort features an outdoor infinity pool, a private beach area, and a complimentary WiFi throughout the property. The room at The Bellevue Resort features a balcony and a seating area, air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a personal safe, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, and a mini-bar. The private bathrooms come with a hairdryer, bathrobe, and free toiletries.

The hotel operates a 24-hour front desk that can assist with luggage storage, laundry and babysitting services. A tour desk and business center is provided for convenience. Guests can request for massage service and also rent a car to explore the island.
