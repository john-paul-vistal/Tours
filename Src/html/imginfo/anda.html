ANDA DE BORACAY BEACH

POBLACION, ANDA, 6311 BOHOL

OPEN 24 HOURS

INFORMATION

Anda de Boracay is one of the best beaches in Bohol. It is known for its fine and white sand. The scenery also look like Boracay that's why it is named as Anda De Boracay. It is located 1.5 hours from the Tagbilaran Airport by car. And 2.5 hours from the Tubigon Port by car.

Theirs no need to worry about hotel and restaurant there are lots of places to rent for affordable Prices. They offer the best quality service and also they offer delicious and 100% boholano food that you don't want to miss.

Also, no need to worry about transportation.There are lots of public and private vehicles that you can ride Or rent for an affordable price. There are also lots of souvenir shops if you want to bring some. It is Made by all boholano and made of native materials.
