MAG-ASO FALLS

MARIBOJOC - ANTIQUERA - CATAGBACAN RD, LOON, BOHOL

OPEN 24 HOURS

INFORMATION

Mag-Aso Falls are eight-meter twin waterfalls situated at the bottom of a deep gorge. Turquoise water surrounded by tropical flora and fauna looks like a lost paradise. There are no sounds except the roaring water. Meditative, calm and very natural place. Itâ€™s located away from most of the attractions of Bohol island, still not covered by most of the Bohol tour itineraries. So itâ€™s usually not crowded. There is a good chance to be there alone.

A staircase of 197 steps goes from a ticket office to falls. In dry weather itâ€™s not difficult to go down and up. But the steps are very slippery in case of rain, so itâ€™s better to use a banister to overcome this path. In the bottom there is only a small area near the water.

Normally the place is quite safe. You can swim at Mag-Aso Falls, however take care, the main pool is quite deep under the falls. It does get shallower further away. For the more adventurous experience you can walk or climb up the far side of the gorge and make a leap in to the pool below. Be careful with slippery rocks when you go into the water or climb up to cliffs.
